Oatmeal

Description

Make a healthy chocolate breakfast in just 7 minutes! This Chocolate Oatmeal will satisfy your cravings, and keep you full for hours. Sweetened with banana it's easy to make vegan & gluten-free.

Ingredients

  • 100g Oats
  • 200g Milk
  • 1 Banana
  • 15g Dark Chocolate Chunks
  • 30g Blueberries
  • 1Tbps Peanut Butter

Steps

  1. Start heating the milk, and let it sit to just before it starts to boil
  2. In a bowl, mash the banana and add the oats
  3. Pour the milk in the bowl and mix well
  4. Add the toppings and enjoy your delicious breakfast
